The role:

The Engineering Lead is a key player in our Agile development process, driving technical excellence and leading a cross-functional team of engineers to deliver high-quality software solutions. This role requires a deep understanding of Agile methodologies (Scrum), strong technical expertise, exceptional leadership skills, and a forward-looking grasp of AI-augmented software development practices. The Engineering Lead will collaborate closely with Deputy Head of Engineering, Scrum Masters, and other stakeholders to prioritize and execute on initiatives that drive business value — increasingly leveraging AI-assisted tooling to accelerate delivery and improve engineering outcomes. In this role you will be reporting to the Deputy Head of Engineering.

Engineering Lead should be highly proficient in .NET core, c#, with deep understanding of its architecture and best practices. They should provide technical guidance, mentorship, and code reviews to ensure high-quality, scalable, and maintainable .NET Core applications.

Engineering Lead should possess a deep understanding of Azure DevOps including version control, continuous integration (CI), continuous deployment (CD), release management, and monitoring. They should use Azure DevOps to streamline the development process, automate repetitive task and improve overall efficiency.

Engineering Lead should demonstrate a strong understanding of AI-augmented software development including the effective use of AI coding assistants (e.g. GitHub Copilot, Claude Code), AI-powered code review workflows, prompt engineering for developer tooling, and the integration of LLM-based agents into the SDLC. They should understand how to embed AI across the development lifecycle — from requirements refinement and code generation through to automated testing, PR review, and incident analysis — while maintaining quality gates, human oversight, and responsible AI practices.
